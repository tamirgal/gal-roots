## Biography
SASSON KHALIFA: the ancestor of the Sasson family. This document is the most ancient official document of the ancestor of the Sasson family. It certifies, that Mr. SASSON KHALIFA, born in 1831, in [[Places/Baghdad]], is registered in the French Embassy in [[Places/Cairo]], in the Tunisian Register, on the 16th of September 1881. Then follow the names of his children and birth dates. Born in 1831, in [[Places/Baghdad]] ([[Places/Iraq]]). Moneychanger and jeweler. Married to Semha Meyer and living in the Jewish quarter in [[Places/Baghdad]] with 5 children. In 1881, all the family immigrated to [[Places/Egypt]] and registered at the French Embassy, in the Tunisian register under French protectorate. Original document with Elie Patan, in the family's folder.

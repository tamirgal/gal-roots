## Biography

At the age of 20, he leaves [[Places/Iraq]], immigrates in [[Places/Egypt]], and lives in the town of Tantah. Married to Helene Mizrahi 17-07-1917,… the family lives in Tantah, where 9 children are born. In 1943, the family moves and lives in [[Places/Cairo]]. In 1949, the family leaves [[Places/Egypt]], immigrates in [[Places/Israel]] and lives in Ramleh.

## Biography
EMILIE COHEN SASSON: born in [[Places/Cairo]], the 24th of August 1916… Married to Albert Sasson, in [[Places/Cairo]] on the 28th of February 1937. In 1982, she left [[Places/Egypt]] with her husband, and immigrated to [[Places/Israel]], and lived in the city of [[Places/Holon]]. After the death of her husband Albert in January 1993, she lived in a home for aged people in the city of "[[Places/Holon]]", untill she died on 2 May 2004

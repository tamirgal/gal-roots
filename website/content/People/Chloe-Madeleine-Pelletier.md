## Biography
Chloe,.. born in Rochester, [[Places/New York]]. Chloe loves soccer, pizza and playing silly with her friends! We call her the ice storm baby because the week she was born a great big ice storm hit Rochester, [[Places/New York]]. The city practically shut down due to falling tree branches on power lines. Without heat or electricity, we had to spend the week before Chlo?'s birth at our good friends' Ruth Gross and Hans Kellner. The experience was exciting and the city was dazzling all covered in ice. Thus was born a most delightful child! Her speed, her imagination and her inexhaustible energy coupled with her unswerving sense of justice and her love of beauty have made her the apple of our eyes. May you all have a chance to get to know her and listen to her play the violin or the piano. She plays with heart, with intelligence and with a speed that has made even her better teachers respectful! Chloe's middle name, Madeleine, derives from that of her paternal grandmother, Madeleine Boucher Pelletier, a woman of so much kindness and profound inner beauty. We chose her first name, Chlo?, because, pronounced in English -- as it would often be in the United States -- it sounded like a resounding and joyful French" oui," or YES! and pronounced in French it would ring out a gleeful and boisterous "oh?"!

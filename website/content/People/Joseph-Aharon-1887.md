## Biography
Joseph Aharon born in 1887, married to Berta Farkas in 1908, died in Nazi extermination camp of [[Places/Auschwitz]] in 1944. Joseph, the elder son of David Aharon, Moved from Tyacev to Sekernice, small town near by , where he met his wife , Berta Farkas and married her in 1908. .. 8 children born to the couple which decided to move again, this time to Komarno (today In Slovakia). In May 1944, Joseph was taken, together with his family to [[Places/Auschwitz]] extermination camp, where he found his death by the Nazis.
